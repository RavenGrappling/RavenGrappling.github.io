As a visitor, I was attracted to Raven Grappling because the website, schedule, pricing, etc. are clear (unlike many other gyms). Having attended, I was impressed by Dmytro's expertise in other martial arts, such as wrestling and judo, which he draws from to coach BJJ. I believe this leads to a far more effective BJJ style.
Additionally, the facility is clean, open-feeling, and there are towels ever available for drying off after a tiring round.
