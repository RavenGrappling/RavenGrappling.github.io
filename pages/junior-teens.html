Junior/Teens Brazilian Jiu Jitsu
Ages 10-14

Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu class for teenagers is designed to provide a safe, supportive, and engaging environment where young martial artists can learn and grow. This program focuses on teaching essential BJJ techniques while promoting physical fitness, self-discipline, and confidence.

{% include leads_form.html program="junior-teens" %}

What to Expect

Structured Classes: Each class includes a warm-up, technique instruction, drilling, and sparring, providing a balanced approach to learning and practicing BJJ.

Expert Instruction: Our knowledgeable instructors create a positive atmosphere, offering personalized feedback and guidance to help each student progress at their own pace.

Positive Environment: Students will build friendships and connections with their peers, fostering a sense of belonging and teamwork within our supportive school environment.

Progress Tracking: Participants will have the opportunity to set goals and track their progress, culminating in the chance to earn promotions and belts that recognize their hard work and dedication.

Benefits of the Program

Self-Defense Skills: Students will learn practical self-defense techniques that empower them to protect themselves in real-life situations, fostering a sense of safety and confidence.

Physical Fitness: BJJ is a full-body workout that improves strength, endurance, flexibility, and coordination, which will enhance their overall physical health while they are having fun.

Mental Resilience: BJJ teaches valuable lessons in perseverance and problem-solving, as students learn to adapt and think critically in challenging scenarios, both during practice and in everyday life.

Community and Friendship: The class structure fosters a supportive environment where students can make new friends, build teamwork skills, and learn the importance of respect and sportsmanship.
