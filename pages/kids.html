Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu: (Ages 5 - 9)

Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) class for kids is designed to introduce young martial artists to the fundamentals of this dynamic and engaging martial art.

With a focus on safety, respect, and fun, our classes create a supportive environment where children can learn essential skills while building confidence and friendships.

{% include leads_form.html program="kids" %}

What to Expect

Structured Curriculum: Classes are designed with clear goals and objectives, focusing on age-appropriate techniques and drills.

Focus on Safety: We prioritize safety through proper instruction, ensuring that all movements are taught in a way that minimizes the risk of injury.

Progressive Skill Development: Students will progress through belt ranks, setting achievable goals that motivate them to continue learning and improving.

Positive Learning Environment: Our classes foster a supportive atmosphere where children can express themselves, make mistakes, and grow at their own pace.

Benefits of the Program

Self-Defense Skills: Kids will learn practical self-defense techniques to be able to protect themselves, boosting their confidence in handling challenging situations.

Improved Physical Fitness: BJJ promotes strength, flexibility, coordination, and overall fitness, helping kids develop healthy habits from a young age.

Discipline and Focus: The structured environment of BJJ encourages kids to cultivate discipline, patience, and focus, skills that translate into success in academics and other areas of life.

Social Skills and Teamwork: Through partner drills and group activities, children will develop important social skills, build friendships, and learn the value of teamwork and respect for others.
