Adults Beginner Program Ages 15+

Our Beginner Adults Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) class is designed for individuals new to the art of grappling and self-defense. This program provides a welcoming and supportive environment where participants can learn foundational techniques, improve their fitness, and build confidence.

{% include leads_form.html program="adult-beginner" %}

What to Expect

Structured Curriculum: Each class will typically include a warm-up, technique instruction, drilling, and live sparring (rolling), allowing you to apply what you’ve learned in a safe environment.

Fundamental Techniques: You’ll learn essential techniques, including basic positions, escapes, submissions, and sweeps, providing a solid foundation for your BJJ journey.

Safe Training Environment: We prioritize safety and respect in our training space, allowing you to practice techniques with partners of varying skill levels while minimizing the risk of injury.

Supportive Community: Join a welcoming community of fellow beginners who share your interest in learning and improving, making the journey enjoyable and motivating.

Benefits of the Program

Improved Fitness: Enhance your strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance through engaging and challenging workouts.

Self-Defense Skills: Gain practical self-defense techniques that empower you to handle real-life situations with confidence and awareness.

Stress Relief: Experience a natural outlet for stress and anxiety, promoting overall mental well-being through physical activity and mindfulness.

Social Connections: Meet new friends and build a supportive community of like-minded individuals, fostering relationships that extend beyond the mat.
